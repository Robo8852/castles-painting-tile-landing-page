# Contract Addendum: Non-Technical Client Provisions
## Castle's Custom Painting & Tile - Backend Handover

### Section 8.2: Non-Technical Client Support Services

**Purpose:** This addendum modifies Section 8.1 to accommodate the client's non-technical background while maintaining compliance with the original agreement terms.

#### Simplified Account Management

**Developer-Assisted Setup:**
- Developer creates all required accounts (Encore.dev, Resend.com) using client's email and billing information
- Developer handles all technical configuration and setup
- Client retains full ownership and control of all accounts
- Developer receives temporary access for setup purposes only

**Account Ownership Timeline:**
```
Phase 1 (Setup):     Developer handles setup using client's billing
Phase 2 (Handover):  Client receives full access and ownership
Phase 3 (Support):   Optional developer maintenance available
```

#### Client-Friendly Handover Process

**What Developer Provides:**
1. **Simple One-Page Guide** - Non-technical instructions for daily operations
2. **Video Walkthroughs** - Visual instructions for admin panel usage
3. **Emergency Contact Sheet** - Who to call for what type of problem
4. **Live Training Session** - 1-hour Zoom call to demonstrate key functions
5. **Password Manager Setup** - Secure storage of all login information

**What Client Receives:**
- Complete ownership of all accounts and data
- Ability to make basic changes without technical knowledge
- Clear instructions for handling common issues
- Emergency support procedures for critical problems

#### Ongoing Support Options

**Included Support (First 7 Days):**
- Bug fixes and technical issues
- Emergency procedures for website downtime
- Basic troubleshooting assistance
- Response time: Within 4 business hours

**Optional Maintenance Packages:**

**Basic Maintenance - $50/month:**
- Website uptime monitoring
- Security updates and patches
- Monthly system health checks
- Email/phone support during business hours
- Response time: Within 24 business hours

**Premium Maintenance - $100/month:**
- All Basic Maintenance features PLUS:
- Content updates (text changes, photo uploads)
- Performance optimization
- Analytics and reporting
- Next business day emergency support
- Response time: Within 4 business hours

#### Modified Responsibilities

**Client Responsibilities:**
1. Provide billing information for service accounts
2. Pay monthly service fees directly to Encore.dev/Resend.com
3. Contact developer for technical support or maintenance packages
4. Maintain basic website security (don't share passwords)

**Developer Responsibilities:**
1. Set up all technical infrastructure using client's billing
2. Provide non-technical documentation and training
3. Offer ongoing support packages at optional cost
4. Ensure smooth transition to client ownership

#### Financial Arrangements

**One-Time Project Fee:** $1,500 (unchanged from original contract)

**Third-Party Service Costs (Client Responsibility):**
- Encore.dev hosting: $0-20/month depending on traffic
- Resend.com email: $0/month (up to 3,000 emails)
- Domain registration: $15/year (if applicable)

**Optional Support Costs:**
- Basic Maintenance: $50/month
- Premium Maintenance: $100/month

**Payment Terms for Support Services:**
- Billed monthly in advance
- 30-day notice for cancellation
- No long-term contracts required

#### Emergency Procedures

**Critical Issues (Website Down):**
- Client calls or texts developer (business hours 9am-6pm, Mon-Fri)
- Developer responds within 4 business hours
- Resolution within 24 business hours (covered under warranty or maintenance)

**Non-Critical Issues:**
- Client emails developer
- Developer responds within 24 business hours
- Resolution within 3-5 business days

#### Training and Documentation

**Live Training Session (1 Hour):**
- Admin panel navigation and usage
- Contact form management
- Basic troubleshooting
- Emergency procedures

**Video Documentation:**
- 10-minute admin panel walkthrough
- 5-minute contact form usage guide
- 5-minute emergency procedures overview

**Written Documentation:**
- One-page quick reference guide
- Emergency contact information sheet
- Monthly cost estimate breakdown

#### Technology Simplification

**For Client Understanding:**
- **"Hosting"** = Keeping website online (like rent for internet space)
- **"Database"** = Digital filing cabinet for customer information
- **"Email Service"** = Automatic email delivery from contact form
- **"Admin Panel"** = Simple website to view customer messages

#### Success Metrics

**What Constitutes Successful Handover:**
1. Client can access and use admin panel independently
2. Client understands emergency procedures
3. All technical systems functioning properly
4. Client comfortable with monthly cost expectations
5. Client knows who to contact for different types of issues

#### Exit Strategy

**If Client Chooses No Ongoing Support:**
- All documentation provided for self-management
- 7-day warranty period still applies
- Developer available for future paid projects
- No obligation for continued technical support

**If Client Chooses Maintenance Package:**
- Priority support for all technical issues
- Proactive monitoring and maintenance
- Content updates included (Premium package)
- Peace of mind for non-technical client
